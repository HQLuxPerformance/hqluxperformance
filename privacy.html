Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

HQ Lux Performance ("we," "us," or "our") operates the HQ Lux Performance mobile application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including name, email address, phone number, vehicle information, and payment details. We also collect location data to match customers with nearby mechanics and to provide real-time tracking during service.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to facilitate bookings, process payments via Stripe, send push notifications about your service, and improve platform performance. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Location Data

The app collects location data in the foreground to show nearby mechanics and track service progress. Location is not collected in the background when the app is closed.

Payment Information

Payments are processed securely through Stripe. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage, Stripe for payment processing, and Google Maps for location services. Each service has its own privacy policy.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
